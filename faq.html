Frequently Asked Questions

What is Promptly Board? +

Promptly Board is a free, curated directory of the best AI tools available today. We organize tools across 10 categories including chatbots, image generation, coding, writing, video, audio, productivity, research, design, and education.

Is Promptly Board free to use? +

Yes! Promptly Board is completely free. No account, no sign-up, and no payment is required. Just browse, search, and discover AI tools.

How do I search for a specific AI tool? +

Use the search bar on the homepage to type the name or description of the tool you are looking for. You can also filter by category using the category buttons, and sort by rating, name, or date added.

How can I submit an AI tool? +

Visit our Submit a Tool page. Fill in the tool name, URL, description, and category. Our team will review your submission and add it to the directory if it meets our quality standards.

How are tools rated? +

Tools are rated on a scale of 1 to 5 based on factors including features, ease of use, free tier availability, community feedback, and overall quality. Ratings are reviewed and updated periodically.

What does "Freemium" mean? +

Freemium means the tool offers a free tier with limited features, and paid plans for additional features or higher usage limits. Most tools listed on Promptly Board have usable free tiers.

How often is the directory updated? +

We update the directory regularly as new AI tools are released. Community submissions help us discover tools faster. Follow us on social media for update announcements.

Can I use Promptly Board in my language? +

Yes! Promptly Board supports 8 languages: English, Korean, Japanese, Chinese, Spanish, French, German, and Portuguese. Use the language selector in the navigation bar to switch languages.

How do I report a broken link or incorrect information? +

Please email us at taeshinkim11@gmail.com with the tool name and details about the issue. We will fix it promptly.
