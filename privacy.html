Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Promptly Board ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website at promptly-board.vercel.app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal, non-personal information to improve our service:

  • Visit Data: Timestamp, page visited, browser user agent, language preference, and timezone. This data does not personally identify you.
  • Tool Submissions: If you submit a tool, we collect the tool name, URL, description, category, and optionally your email address.
  • Local Storage: We use browser local storage for visitor counter, language preference, and dark mode preference. This data stays on your device.

3. No User Accounts

Promptly Board does not require user accounts. We do not collect passwords, personal names, or billing information.

4. How We Use Information

  • To display visitor counts on the website.
  • To review and add submitted AI tools to the directory.
  • To improve website content and user experience.
  • To detect and prevent spam submissions.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Sheets: To store tool submissions and visit logs.
  • Vercel: For website hosting.
  • Adsterra: For displaying advertisements. Adsterra may use cookies.
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements. Google may use cookies and tracking.

6. Cookies

Promptly Board itself does not set cookies. However, third-party ad services (Adsterra, Google AdSense) may set cookies for ad targeting and analytics. Please refer to their respective privacy policies.

7. GDPR Compliance

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII). All data collected is non-personal and used solely for site functionality. If you have submitted an email with a tool submission and wish to have it removed, contact us.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect collected data. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at taeshinkim11@gmail.com.
