Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Promptly Board ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you visit promptly-board.vercel.app, how that information is used, and what rights you have regarding that information. Please read this policy carefully. By using Promptly Board, you agree to the practices described here.

Promptly Board is a read-only AI prompt library and directory. We do not operate user accounts, subscription services, or payment systems. The data we collect is minimal, non-personal, and limited to what is necessary to operate the site and display advertising that keeps the service free.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you visit our site:

  • Anonymous Visit Data: When you load a page, we log a timestamp, the page URL visited, your browser's user-agent string, your approximate timezone, and your language preference. This data does not contain your IP address in identifiable form and cannot be used to personally identify you. It is used solely to maintain a visitor counter displayed on the site and to understand which pages and prompts are most popular.
  • Tool Submission Data (Optional): If you use our Submit a Tool form, we collect the tool name, URL, description, category, and optionally an email address you choose to provide. This data is stored in a Google Sheets database used for our moderation workflow. Submission is entirely voluntary — no submission is required to use any part of the site.
  • Browser Local Storage: We store three pieces of data in your browser's local storage: a visit counter value, your language preference, and your dark/light mode preference. This data lives entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. You can clear it at any time through your browser's developer tools or settings.

3. No User Accounts

Promptly Board does not offer user registration or account creation. We do not collect passwords, personal names, billing information, home addresses, or any other personally identifying information as part of normal site operation. The site is designed as a fully read-only, account-free resource, and this design is intentional — it means we simply cannot hold sensitive personal data about our visitors, because we never collect it in the first place.

4. Cookies and Advertising

Promptly Board itself does not set any first-party cookies. However, we display advertisements through two third-party advertising networks: Google AdSense and Adsterra. Both networks may set their own cookies in your browser for the purpose of ad personalization, frequency capping, fraud prevention, and performance measurement. These cookies are governed entirely by the respective third-party privacy policies listed below.

  • Google AdSense: Google uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site and other sites on the internet. You can opt out of Google's use of cookies for ad personalization by visiting Google's Ads Settings. Google's full privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Adsterra: Adsterra may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to deliver and measure advertising. Adsterra's privacy policy is available on their website. You may opt out of interest-based advertising by visiting the NAI opt-out page.

We comply with applicable laws regarding cookie disclosure and, where required by regulation, will implement a cookie consent mechanism. If you are visiting from the European Union or California, please see sections 7 and 8 below for your specific rights.

5. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used for the following purposes only:

  • To display an anonymous visitor counter on the website, showing daily and total visitor counts as a social proof signal.
  • To understand which prompts and categories are most used, so we can prioritize adding more content in those areas.
  • To review and moderate tool and prompt submissions.
  • To detect and prevent automated spam submissions to our submission form.
  • To improve website content, navigation, and user experience based on aggregate usage patterns.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your information with third parties for their own marketing purposes. We do not use your information to create personal profiles or behavioral advertising campaigns.

6. Third-Party Services

In addition to advertising networks, we use the following third-party services to operate the site:

  • Vercel: Our website is hosted on Vercel's infrastructure. Vercel may collect standard web server logs as part of hosting operations. Vercel's privacy policy applies to that data.
  • Google Sheets: Anonymous visit log data and tool submission data are stored in a Google Sheets spreadsheet accessible only to our team. Google's privacy policy applies to data stored in Google's systems.

7. GDPR Rights (European Union and EEA Residents)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, rectify, erase, and restrict processing of your personal data, as well as the right to data portability and to object to processing. Because Promptly Board does not collect personally identifiable information from standard site visitors, most of these rights apply primarily to submission data (where you voluntarily provided an email address). To exercise any GDPR right, please contact us at taeshinkim11@gmail.com and we will respond within 30 days.

8. CCPA Rights (California Residents)

California residents have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to request deletion, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. Promptly Board does not sell personal information. The only personal data we may hold about a California resident is a voluntarily submitted email address from our tool submission form. To request access to or deletion of this data, contact us at taeshinkim11@gmail.com.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the limited data we collect. Our site is served over HTTPS, which encrypts data in transit. Submission data stored in Google Sheets is protected by Google account security and is accessible only to our team. No method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. However, given that we do not collect sensitive personal information, the risk to our users from a data breach is minimal.

10. Children's Privacy

Promptly Board is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at taeshinkim11@gmail.com and we will delete that information promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or applicable law. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of Promptly Board after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at: taeshinkim11@gmail.com. We aim to respond to all privacy inquiries within 5 business days.
