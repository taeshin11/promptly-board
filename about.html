About Promptly Board

Our Mission

Promptly Board is a free, curated directory of the best AI tools available today. Our mission is to help everyone discover, explore, and use artificial intelligence tools that can enhance their work, creativity, and productivity.

What We Do

We carefully curate and organize AI tools across 10 categories including chatbots, image generation, coding assistants, writing tools, video creation, audio and music, productivity, research, design, and education. Each tool is rated, described, and categorized to help you find exactly what you need.

Why Promptly Board?

  • 100% Free to Use - No sign-up, no paywalls, no hidden costs.
  • Curated Quality - Every tool is reviewed before listing.
  • Always Updated - New tools are added regularly via community submissions.
  • Community Driven - Anyone can submit an AI tool for consideration.
  • Multi-Language - Available in 8 languages for global accessibility.

Community Submissions

Know a great AI tool that should be listed? Submit it here and our team will review it. We welcome contributions from the community!
